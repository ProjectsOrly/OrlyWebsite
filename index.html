Q&A

How do I transfer files?

Once you have downloaded the desktop application, you will be able to transfer files from your mobile device to your computer by simply clicking the "Start Upload" button on the mobile app. Just remember to adjust the settings to your liking first :)

I'm having trouble connecting my devices. What can I do?

If you're having trouble creating a connection between your devices, ensure that they are both connected to the same Wi-Fi network. If that doesn't work, it's possible that your firewall is blocking the connection. In that case, try adjusting your firewall settings to allow the connection.
Also if you're attempting to connecting with IOS device, make sure that local network permission is enabled in the settings.

Will the app delete any files from my mobile device?

No, the app does not have the ability to delete any files from your mobile device, nor do we wish to take any responsibility for such actions.

I'm receiving a "Folder does not exist" error

There are a few possibilities for this error message. It could be that the folder truly does not exist, or perhaps the folder was renamed. Additionally, it's possible that the folder is located in a location that the desktop app cannot access, such as OneDrive or a similar service.

I cant view HEIC format files

Is the app secure?

Yes, Your files are transferred directly from your computer to your phone via a local WiFi connection. This means that your files remain solely within your personal devices and are not stored on any external servers or clouds, Orly does not take any responsibility for any files that are transferred.

I accidentally deleted my connection

If you have accidentally deleted the connection from your mobile or computer, you can simply create a new connection and adjust the sync date in your mobile settings to match the previous connection. This will allow you to resume where you left off with ease.
